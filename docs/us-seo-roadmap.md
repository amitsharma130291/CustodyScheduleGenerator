# CustodyBuilder US SEO Roadmap

CustodyBuilder should continue targeting US parents, caregivers, mediators, and family-law researchers who are comparing custody schedules, parenting plans, visitation schedules, parenting time calculators, and printable co-parenting tools.

## Top US State-Specific Opportunities

1. California
   - High search demand around parenting plans, visitation schedules, time-share percentage calculations, and holiday parenting schedules.
   - Strong fit for pages explaining printable custody schedules, parenting time calendars, and co-parenting plan templates.

2. Texas
   - Strong demand around standard possession orders, expanded standard possession, visitation schedules, holiday schedules, and parenting time.
   - Future pages should explain general planning concepts without giving legal advice.

3. Florida
   - Strong demand around parenting plans, time-sharing schedules, school-year calendars, and holiday schedules.
   - Content should use Florida-friendly terminology such as parenting plan and time-sharing while remaining clear for national users.

4. New York
   - Strong demand around visitation schedules, custody calendars, parenting time, and alternating weekends.
   - Good opportunity for examples that show school-year and holiday rotations.

5. Illinois, Pennsylvania, Ohio, Georgia, North Carolina, Michigan, Arizona, and Washington
   - Useful second-wave opportunities for custody schedule examples, parenting plan templates, and visitation calendar pages.
   - Prioritize states with high population and clear search demand before creating long-tail state pages.

- `/california-custody-schedule`

Each state page should include:

- Plain-English overview of custody schedule planning in that state.
- Common parenting time schedules: 50/50, 60/40, 70/30, 80/20, 2-2-3, 2-2-5-5, 5-2-2-5, and alternating weekends.
- Printable custody calendar examples.
- Holiday schedule considerations.
- Custody percentage or parenting time calculation guidance.
- Clear disclaimer that the page is educational planning content, not legal advice.
- Internal links to national schedule pages, templates, calculators, and the parenting plan template.

- Add state-specific CTAs in future blog posts that point back to the custody schedule generator.
- Add a future "Custody Schedules by State" hub once at least four state pages are live.
- Link holiday-focused state content to `/holiday-custody-schedule` and `/holiday-visitation-schedule`.

## Content Quality Guidelines

- Use US English and US custody terminology.
- Avoid legal conclusions and state-specific legal advice.
- Keep pages geo-neutral unless the page is explicitly state-specific.
- Use examples, calendars, comparison tables, FAQs, and internal links.
- Keep disclaimers concise and visible, especially on state pages.
- Avoid thin pages; target 1,200-2,000 useful words for state landing pages.
